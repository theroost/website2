__ About
The Roost

The Roost is a community of independent creative people sharing space and ideas. We work autonomously and also collaborate occasionally.

We co-work and play out of an open-plan studio based in the heart of Hunter St Mall, Newcastle. We’re a mix of emerging creatives and established industry mentors. Among us are graphic designers, illustrators, marketing professionals, photographers, event planners, writers and others working in digital media.

We’re a mix of emerging creatives and established industry mentors.

As members, we use The Roost as a base for developing from graduates to professionals, starting and running our businesses, meeting clients, hosting events and making cool things happen as a result of joining forces. If you have a project that requires creative input, we’ve got a nest of talent.

We’re not about being in competition; rather we’re committed to supporting the energy and inspiration of our local creative industry, providing avenues for professional development and supporting each other’s endeavours.

Our doors are always open, so if you want to come and check out who’s around doing interesting things, talk to us or work with us, then please – get in touch.
